01

Layout with parametric cabinets

Drag base, wall and tall units from the ArchiWood library. Place corners and special units, then stretch and resize them without breaking drilling or structure.

02

Choose structure & connections

Decide on back-panel style, plinths, wall fillers, drawers, and connection methods – cam-locks, dowels, Lamello, hidden fasteners or mortise-and-tenon – and even mix methods in one project.

CAPABILITIES

What makes ArchiWood different?

The following modules turn SketchUp into a complete cabinet engineering system.

Parametric cabinet & door library

A rich, fully parametric library for base units, wall units, tall cabinets, corner solutions and non-standard pieces. All sizes, offsets and rules are editable and can be customized to your factory standards.

Design = breakdown

What you see is what you manufacture: every panel, groove, notch and hole is already in the 3D model. When design is finished, you simply add or confirm hardware and generate reports – no manual 2D redrawing.

Rich connection technology

Support for classic cam-lock systems, dowels, Lamello, invisible connectors and traditional mortise-and-tenon. Hybrid workflows are supported – for example cam-locks for most joints combined with Lamello on visible faces.

Fast material swapping

Doors and carcases often use different finishes. ArchiWood lets you replace materials per door set, per carcase, or globally with a few clicks so you can try many combinations while the structure stays unchanged.

Free-form cabinet cutting

Cut corners around columns and beams by simply selecting the cabinet and the obstruction. The structure remains production-ready with correct panels and drilling. For special cases, draw your own cutting profile and let ArchiWood generate the resulting geometry.

Automatic checking & reports

After modelling, run cabinet checks to detect missing backs, wrong grain direction, overlapping drilling, unsupported hardware and more. Then export structured reports for ordering, pricing and production.

Quick parametric editing

Insert mid-panels, change back-panel style, add drawers or change cabinet height and width without distortion. ArchiWood treats the cabinet as a parametric object, not a pile of loose faces.

From 2D elevation to 3D cabinet

Already have 2D facade drawings in SketchUp? ArchiWood can convert them into full parametric 3D cabinets in one step, so the time spent on elevation design is never wasted.

LayOut documentation in one click

Need assembly views and dimensioned drawings? Send annotated cabinet views to LayOut automatically and focus on engineering instead of repeating drawing work.
