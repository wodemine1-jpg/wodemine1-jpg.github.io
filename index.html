ABOUT ARCHIWOOD

A Complete Design-to-Production System

Built on SketchUp’s intuitive modeling engine, ArchiWood offers a fully parametric cabinet system, a free-flow design environment, real-time visualization for clients, and direct production output.

WYSIWYG – What You See Is What You Produce

The design model is the production model. Hardware, drilling, structures and materials update automatically. No remodeling, no duplication, no manual processing.

Direct Production Output

One-click export of BOM, labels, reports, nesting layouts, and CNC code. Fully compatible with factories worldwide.

Suitable for Designers, Studios & Factories

Simple enough for designers, powerful enough for manufacturing plants. Works for batch production and fully custom projects.

KEY FEATURES

Core Capabilities of ArchiWood

Designed for real production, not just visualization.

Parametric Cabinet Library

Base cabinets, wall cabinets, tall cabinets, corner cabinets, wardrobe units, special modules— all fully parametric and factory-customizable.

Instant Dimension Editing

Resize complete kitchens in seconds. Structures, drilling and hardware update automatically with zero deformation.

Advanced Material Control

Doors and boxes can be replaced independently or matched together with one click. Built-in wood-grain materials accelerate client presentation.

Free Cabinet Cutting

Automatically cut cabinets around columns or beams while keeping all internal structures production-ready. Custom cut-shapes are supported.

Multi-Process CNC Support

Traditional 3-in-1, hidden fasteners, Lamello, mortise-tenon joints—mix any process within the same project.

Automatic Error Checking

Detect missing parts, invalid thickness, drilling conflicts and structure issues before sending orders to production.

WORKFLOW

From Design to CNC Production

1. Build Layout in SketchUp

Drag and drop modules to form a full kitchen or wardrobe in minutes.

2. Adjust Parameters & Processes

Modify thickness, materials, hardware, structure rules—everything updates instantly.

3. Auto Nesting & Validation

Generate optimized sheet layouts, labels, BOM and production documents.

4. Export CNC Files

Output G-code, XXL or NC files directly to your machines. No manual programming required.
